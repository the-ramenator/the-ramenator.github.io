Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a painting?

The time required to complete a painting can vary greatly depending on the size, complexity, and style of the artwork. Generally, smaller paintings may take a few days to a week, while larger or more intricate pieces can take several weeks or even months to finish.

What materials do you use for your paintings?

I primarily use high-quality acrylic paints on canvas for my paintings. However, I am also skilled in using oil paints and mixed media techniques, depending on the specific requirements of the project and the preferences of my clients.

Do you offer custom paintings?

Yes, I offer custom paintings tailored to your specific vision and requirements. Whether you have a particular subject in mind or want to collaborate on a unique piece, I am happy to work with you to create a personalized artwork that meets your expectations.
